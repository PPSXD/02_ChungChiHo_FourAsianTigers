History

When the Avenue of Stars was first established, the palm prints were carved directly into the ground. When the Avenue of Stars was renovated, the handprints were moved to the adjacent Garden of Stars for display. After renovation in 2019, the paw prints were moved from the ground to the waterfront railings, and digital elements were added. Visitors can scan the QR code with their mobile phones to watch short films related to the star.

For artists who do not have their fingerprints left, their appearance will be made up of stars on the introduction page instead of their fingerprints.
